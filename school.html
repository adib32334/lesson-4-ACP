About My School

Mymensingh stigious educational institutions in Bangladesh. Located in Mymensingh city, the school holds a significant place in the history of education in the region. Here's an overview:
History
Establishment: Mymensingh Zilla School was established in 1853 during the British colonial period, making it one of the earliest formal schools in Bangladesh. It was initially set up to provide quality education to local students and has since maintained a reputation for academic excellence.
Campus
The school is situated in the heart of Mymensingh city. The campus includes historical buildings, classrooms, a large playground, and facilities for extracurricular activities.
Academics
The school caters to students from grades 3 to 10 and prepares them for the Secondary School Certificate (SSC) examination under the national curriculum. It is renowned for its outstanding performance in public examinations and competitive academic environment.
Extracurricular Activities
Mymensingh Zilla School offers various clubs and activities, including debate, science fairs, cultural programs, and sports. Students are encouraged to participate in national and international competitions.
Notable Alumni
Over the years, the school has produced many distinguished personalities in various fields, including politics, academia, literature, and science.
Cultural Significance
The school plays an important role in the cultural and intellectual life of Mymensingh, hosting events such as annual sports days, cultural programs, and inter-school competitions.
